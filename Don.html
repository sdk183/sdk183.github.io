Don Tacos


Don Tacos is a Mexican restaurant that serves tacos and other mexican cuisines. I came across this restaurant when I was looking around for local restaurants that sell tacos near me. It's not the cleanest restauarnt as the building is old and worn down, but the food was decent and at a decent price point.

My Review

The restauarnt wasn't the cleanest nor the prettiest, but to be fair, the building was old and worn down. I went around 9pm after I finished my night classes. I ordered three al pastor tacos, got some sides like cilantro, onions, and sauces. The sauces weren't the spiciest, and the meat was very chewy and not as tender, though it was almost closing time. The corn tortilla was soggy, but held the meat pretty well.

My rating: 3.5/5

Located at: 1916 E Belt Line Rd A, Carrollton, TX 75006
