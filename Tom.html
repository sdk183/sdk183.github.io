Tomyumkung Thai Cafe


Tomyumkung Thai Cafe is a Thai restaurant that serves staple Thai dishes such as pad thai, thai fried rice, green curry, and other traditional Thai dishes.

My Review

When I walked inside the restaurant, I knew that it was styled around the Thailand culture as it had many trinkets, statues, and stylistic details that reflected the culture. I ordered the thai fried rice with chicken and when it came out, it was piping hot as it freshly made. The thai fried rice was amazing, one of the best thai fried rice I ever had, and I have been wanting to go back and try it again. The dish was about $15 with tip included and the portion size was large enough for me to have left overs. Though the service was lacking as the host was not warm or welcoming, so I had to take that into consideration.

My rating: 4/5

Located at: 3030 N Josey Ln Apt 113, Carrollton, TX 75007
